polimine™

Fully integrated mining automation solution

In addition to systems required by regulations such as instant personnel tracking, gas measurement systems in polimine™ underground mines; It includes additional modules such as automatic fan control, vehicle signaling systems, critical area/area control that will increase the efficiency of your work and optimize your work. consumption and the data it collects is your business. It is a mine automation solution that can be integrated with IT systems. Below we have explained the details of the polymine™ solution.

Güçlü & Ortak Altyapı

Thanks to the common network infrastructure installed by our expert field teams in standards suitable for underground mine conditions, all the systems your business needs work on a single infrastructure. Built on standard TCP/IP protocol, polimine™ solution protects your business from additional infrastructure costs. Thanks to the state-of-the-art firewalls installed on the closed circuit network infrastructure, it is protected against external attacks. Since almost every type of automation system accepted in the world can communicate with TCP/IP protocols, additional automation requests that may be needed in the future can be easily installed on the same infrastructure. In this way, it reduces your communication infrastructure investment costs to a single item. Thanks to fiber optic cable technology, it eliminates the communication problems of large-scale enterprises. Supports Voice and Video communication, enabling you to communicate in real time with your staff across kilometres.

Real-Time Personnel Tracking System

The Ministry of Labour and Social Security stipulated that the mandatory underground personnel monitoring systems should be able to monitor the instantaneous location of underground workers in the mine from a station to be installed on the surface, and that underground and surface units should be able to give two-way alarms in case of danger.

polimine™ CUI enables you to monitor the real-time locations of your employees inside the mine. You can track their positions from a monitoring center set up on the surface, any other computer within the facility, or even from anywhere in the world via the internet. Thanks to real-time communication technology, you can instantly monitor emergencies underground and protect your employees by sending vibrating and illuminated alerts from the surface to the underground area in case of urgent situations.

polimine™ CUI Main Features

  • Real-Time Personnel Tracking
  • Cumulative Display of Underground / Surface Personnel by Area Control
  • Automatic Shift Tracking Thanks to Area Control
  • Emergency Alarm Monitoring
  • Retrospective Location Tracking
  • Retrospective Alarm Monitoring
  • Alarm, Location, Area Reports and Excel and PDF Printouts of these Reports
  • Customisable Dashboards
  • Viewing All Underground Automation Systems on a Single Screen

polimine™ F-Tag

With its vibration, light and sound warning features, it allows you to send a warning to your underground workers in emergency situations.

Underground instant personnel tracking systems have been made compulsory for all underground mines as of 01.01.2017 with the publication of the Official Gazette dated 24.03.2016 and numbered 29663 titled "REGULATION ON THE AMENDMENT OF THE REGULATION ON OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY IN MINING WORKSITES" by the Ministry of Labour and Social Security.

Gas Measurement System

Gas measurement systems, which are mandatory especially in grizzly mines, are integrated with the polimine™ solution and provide service over the same communication infrastructure and reduce the infrastructure investment costs of your business. The measurement stations to be installed in the areas to be measured transfer the data to the central system and allow the gas measurement values to be monitored from the surface station. The stations can operate on their own in case of a possible communication breakdown thanks to the gas level indicator and alarm warning equipment on them. In case of exceeding critical gas threshold levels, you can automatically warn your underground workers and direct them to the nearest exit point. For this process, polimine™ solution can send light and vibration warnings to polimine™ H-Tag devices according to the emergency scenarios you create using instantaneous personnel locations.

Customisable Dashboards

Thanks to polimine™ CUI, you can design the dashboards suitable for your needs as you wish. Thanks to the advanced authorisation interface, you can authorise your designed dashboards to different personnel within the enterprise. While designing your dashboards, you can benefit from tools such as IndoorMap, Gauge, Map, AlertBox, PropertyListBox, Counter on polimine™ CUI.

İş Akış Editörü

Thanks to polimine™ WF, you can easily make the rules suitable for your needs by using the workflow editor. Thanks to its user-friendly visual interface, you can save time and technical labour by making additional requirements that are not in the system but you need by drag-and-drop method. polimine™ real-time triggering mechanism automatically starts the rules you set according to the changing characteristics of your assets. With polimine™ WF, you can create alarm scenarios specific to your needs, produce periodic report outputs and send them as e-mails to the people you specify within the enterprise. In addition, if you wish, you can control your machines and equipment within the enterprise via the cloud.

IT Integration

polimine™ portal easily transfers the data collected from the field to these systems by exchanging data with ERP, MRP systems within the enterprise thanks to API support. This method eliminates the human factor in transferring critical data such as consumption and alarms to ERP and MRP programmes.
