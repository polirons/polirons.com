polimine™ GMS Underground Gas Monitoring System

polimine™ GMS gas measurement system is integrated with polimine™ solution and provides service over the same communication infrastructure, reducing the infrastructure investment costs of your business. The measurement stations to be installed in the areas to be measured transfer the data to the central system and enable the gas measurement values to be monitored from the surface station. The stations can operate on their own in case of a possible communication breakdown thanks to the gas level display and alarm warning equipment on them. If critical gas threshold levels are exceeded, underground workers are automatically warned and can be directed to the nearest exit point.


Features
  • polimineTM Full Integration
  • Visual Emergency Scenarios Prepared with polimineTM WF
  • Directing Employees to Emergency Escape Sections with Vibration and Illuminated Warnings to be Sent to PolimineTM F-Tag in Emergency Situations
  • Standalone / Independent Operation from Central System with Standalone Operation Feature

Measurements of Gas and Other Fields
  • Oxygen (O2)
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO)
  • Hydrogen Sulphide (H2S)
  • Methane (CH4)
  • Ammonia (NH3)
  • Air Flow Velocity
  • Temperature
  • Humidity
