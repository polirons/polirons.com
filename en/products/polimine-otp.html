polimine™ AssetMeter Underground / Aboveground Cumulative Personnel Counter

polimine™ AssetMeter is a led panel display that shows the number of underground and above ground personnel, vehicles, i.e. assets in real time. Thanks to its API support built on TCP/IP layer, it easily communicates with 3rd party programs. By showing full compatibility with the polimine™ system, it instantly reflects the entrances and exits of above-ground and underground personnel in the areas where the system is installed.


Features
  • Show Green and Red Color
  • Real-time communication support
  • Integration with 3rd party software thanks to TCP/IP support
  • compatible with polimine™ Heart Service
  • Protection class IP65

polimine™ UView Underground Video Systems

polimine™ UView transmits the images received from IP cameras suitable for mining conditions to the monitoring centre above ground via fibre infrastructure, allowing you to receive instant images from inside the mine as video or picture. It can be mounted on miner's helmet and/or fixed areas. Thanks to its night vision feature, you can direct your employees in case of a possible lamp breakdown or breakage and transport them to the nearest escape point.


Features
  • Exd I Mb Explosion Proof Rating
  • Stainless steel 304/316L Optional
  • IP67 Protection Class
  • 1280*960 Image Resolution
  • Night Vision
  • 0ºC~+40ºC Operating Temperature

polimine™ UPhone Underground Voice Communication Systems

With polimine™ UPhone system, you can integrate IP telephones suitable for mining conditions with fiber infrastructure and communicate with your employees underground. You can achieve cost and maintenance advantages with polimine™ fiber infrastructure without the need for additional infrastructure investment for communication systems such as push-to-talk or full-duplex operating modes and walkie-talkies.


Features
  • Android™ 8.1 Operating System
  • 2 GB RAM, 16 GB Memory
  • 2.6" Capacitive Multi-Touch Display
  • -20~+60 °C Operating Temperature
  • WiFi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/e/k/r/h; 2.4 Ghz and 5 Ghz Support
  • IP68 Protection Class
  • 8 MP Rear Camera (Optional)

Components

  • Underground / Overground Personnel Cumulative Display Led Panel
